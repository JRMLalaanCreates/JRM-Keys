How to use this

Four steps from picking a key to playing a full progression.

  1. 1 Choose a key — try C, G, D, F, or Bb to start.
  2. 2 Play the highlighted scale notes on the main keyboard, left to right.
  3. 3 Study each family chord card below and play its highlighted notes together, as a triad.
  4. 4 Combine chords into a progression, like I–V–vi–IV or vi–IV–I–V.
